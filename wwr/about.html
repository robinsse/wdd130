At Rexburg Rafting, it is our mission to create a fun bonding experience for your whole group.

History

Rexburg Rafting started in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. It was our way of having fun in nature despite the stress of having to isolate. Rafting on the Snake River was the best medicine.
