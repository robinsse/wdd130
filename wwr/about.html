About Us

At Rexburg Rafting, we’re all about adventure, adrenaline, and unforgettable river experiences. Nestled in the heart of Idaho, we offer guided white water rafting trips on some of the region’s most thrilling rivers. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned paddler, our expert guides ensure a safe, exciting journey through stunning landscapes. With top-notch gear and a passion for the outdoors, Rexburg Rafting is your go-to for making waves and creating memories that last.

History

Rexburg Rafting was founded in 2020, right in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic. With travel restricted and people craving safe outdoor experiences, a small group of local river guides saw an opportunity. Armed with a few rafts, safety gear, and a deep love for Idaho’s rivers, they launched their first season on the South Fork of the Snake River. What started as a modest idea quickly caught on with locals looking for adventure and fresh air.

By the summer of 2021, Rexburg Rafting had grown into a full-fledged operation. Word spread fast about the fun, safe, and scenic trips the team offered. From socially distanced floats to full-throttle whitewater runs, guests kept coming back for more. The company expanded its routes, added new guides, and invested in top-tier equipment. What began as a pandemic-era pivot evolved into a beloved adventure brand rooted in community, passion, and river-tested grit.
