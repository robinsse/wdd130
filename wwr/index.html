Your Information

Rexburg Rafting exists to ignite a spirit of adventure through unforgettable white water experiences in the heart of Idaho. Our purpose is to connect people with nature, offering thrilling yet safe river journeys that showcase the raw beauty of the Snake River and beyond. Whether you’re chasing adrenaline or seeking a peaceful escape, we provide expert-guided trips tailored to all skill levels. We’re passionate about fostering community, promoting outdoor recreation, and creating lasting memories for every guest. At Rexburg Rafting, we believe the river has something powerful to offer—and we're here to help you discover it.

Rexburg Rafting offers a variety of exciting day trips designed to suit every adventurer’s schedule. Whether you're looking for a quick thrill or a full day on the water, we have you covered with options ranging from 2 to 8 hours. Our 2-hour trips are perfect for beginners or those short on time, while our 4 and 6-hour excursions provide a more immersive river experience. For those seeking the ultimate adventure, our 8-hour trip offers a full-day adventure through the most scenic and challenging rapids. Each trip is guided by experienced professionals who prioritize safety and fun, ensuring a memorable experience for all.
